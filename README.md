🎴 ONO — The Classic Game. Reimagined.

Remember the card game that turned family nights into battlegrounds and friendships into rivalries? We took that magic and rebuilt it from the ground up for the smartphone generation.

ONO is a real-time multiplayer card game where every millisecond counts. No waiting. No lag. Just pure, instant card-slamming action.

🔥 WHY ONO?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

⚡ REAL-TIME MULTIPLAYER
Play with 2 to 10 players in private rooms. Share a 6-digit room code with your friends and jump into a game in seconds. Our proprietary delta-sync engine ensures every card play arrives instantly — we're talking ~100 bytes per action, not bloated 20KB payloads.

🎯 OFFICIAL UNO 2018 RULES
Strict rule enforcement you can trust. No-stacking penalties, Wild +4 challenge rules, mandatory draw before pass, and the iconic UNO call system with a 10-second countdown. Forget to call UNO? You're getting penalized.

🃏 MULTI-CARD STAIRCASE PLAYS
Drop multiple cards of the same number in one dramatic move. Watch them fly across the screen in a staggered staircase animation that every player in the room sees.

🎨 CINEMATIC CARD ANIMATIONS
Every card play is a mini spectacle — bezier-curve flight paths, wild card shockwave VFX with particle bursts, elastic slam-in entries, and pulsating neon glows. This isn't just a card game. It's a visual experience.

🏆 WINNER CELEBRATIONS
Cross the finish line and get celebrated with a full-screen victory overlay, confetti explosions, and a medal ceremony worthy of a champion.

⏱️ AFK PROTECTION
40-second turn timer with a visual countdown bar. Go AFK twice? Auto-kicked. No dead lobbies, no wasted time.

📜 DISCARD PILE HISTORY
Tap the discard pile to scroll through every card that's been played. Track your opponents' patterns. Play smarter.

🌙 PREMIUM DARK-MODE UI
Designed for immersion. Deep dark gradients, glassmorphism overlays, neon accents, and a UI that feels like it belongs in 2030.
